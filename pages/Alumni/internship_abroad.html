Internship Abroad

Phnom Penh International University supports students in applying for internships abroad. Gain experience, skills, and international exposure through our partner programs.

Japan Tech Internship

Intern in leading Japanese tech companies and gain experience in software engineering and robotics.

Includes 3-month paid internships with housing support. Open to Computer Science and MIS students. Language training provided before departure.

Germany Medical Program

Medical and nursing students can intern at German hospitals to learn European healthcare standards.

The program offers clinical training, German language immersion, and mentoring with hospital professionals.

Singapore Hospitality Internship

Intern with top hotels in Singapore and enhance your hospitality and front-office management skills.

Ideal for Hotel and Tourism students. The 6-month program includes professional mentorship, free accommodation, and stipend.
