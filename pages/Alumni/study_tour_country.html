Study Tour in the Country

Historical Sites Tour

Discover Cambodia’s rich cultural heritage through guided visits to temples and museums.

This tour includes educational trips to Angkor Wat, National Museum, and local cultural workshops. Learn about ancient civilizations and architecture.

Agriculture Field Trip

Learn about local farming techniques, sustainability, and agro-business through real field experience.

Visit rice fields, meet local farmers, and understand agricultural innovation in Cambodia. Ideal for environmental science and economics students.

University Campus Tour

Explore top universities in Phnom Penh to learn about academic life, programs, and future careers.

Includes guided campus tours, mock lectures, career talks, and student networking sessions with local undergraduates.
