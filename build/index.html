Academic Faculties

Phnom Penh International University (PPIU) has its own important missions in the development of human resources with a real atmosphere of Quality, Excellence, and Innovation in order to prepare students to become professionals and also to be part of national, regional, and global developments. To achieve these missions, PPIU has carefully selected core programs with highly experienced and qualified lecturers to teach students more than just their discipline, dignity, and ethics.

Phnom Penh International University offers foreign language training and professional education at associate, bachelor, master, and doctoral levels. Foreign language training is available at the International Language Center (ILC), which is located within the university.
